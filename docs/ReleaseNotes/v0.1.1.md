# BA Console v0.1.1 – Customers

## Objective

Add the first useful management screen to the Console: Customers.

## Changes

- Searchable customer list.
- Status filters.
- Customer detail panel.
- Placeholder New Customer dialog.
- Dashboard metrics now use the customer sample data.

## Test Plan

1. Open `index.html`.
2. Go to Customers.
3. Search for `curzon`.

5. Test Active, Trial and Paused filters.
6. Click View on a customer and check the detail panel.
7. Click + New Customer and close the placeholder dialog.
8. Check the footer version.
